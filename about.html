Who Are We?

Our Collaborative Vision

Our Tri-Parish Collaborative of Roxbury and Jamaica Plain will be salt, light, and leaven in our communities. As one large family of faith, we celebrate our diversity in unity. The joy, peace, and good works of our members invite others to join with us as disciples of Jesus Christ.. our tri-parish family, through God's love, will generate an environment where vocations flourish and our reverent, vibrant, and prayerful worship nurtures us so that in every part of our lives we give thanks and glory to God.
