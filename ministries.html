St. Vincent de Paul Society

The St. Vincent de Paul society meets every Thursday from 6:30 to 7:30pm at Fr. Thomas Hall.

Through your generosity we are able to provide food vouchers to Stop & Shop for those in need.

Once a month we provide laundry and dish detergent, shampoo, hand soap and tooth paste.

We appeal to your generosity to leave some of these items at the back of the church, so that we may continue to do this.

Saint Vincent de Paul is not able to accept used clothing. For more information, contact the rectory @ 617-524-0240

or stthosaq@comcast.net.


Visits to Shattuck Shelter

A group of St. Thomas volunteers brings and serves dessert to the guests at the Shattuck Shelter

on the third Thursday of each month.

We welcome donations of desserts and/or snacks. Please leave them in the rectory vestibule (the side door to the right of the front door of the rectory) by 5 PM on the 3rd Thursday of the month.

For more information, contact the rectory @ 617-524-0240 or stthosaq@comcast.net.
